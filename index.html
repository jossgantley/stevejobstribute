Steve Jobs

The man with a vision "a computer for the rest of us".

Steve's timeline:

  • 1955: Steve Jobs was born on February 25th, 1955 in San Francisco. Paul and Clara Jobs become Steve's parents after his biological parents gave him up for adoption.
  • 1968: Completed an internship with Bill Hewlett after Steve called Bill looking for parts to build a frequency counter. He got more than just the parts!
  • 1972: Completed one semester at Reed College in Portland before dropping out while still attending classes that interested him.
  • 1974: While working at Atari, Jobs employed Steve Wozniak to work with him on a prototype pong game which later became "Breakout".
  • 1976: Jobs co-founded Apple I computer with Wozniak and Ronald Wayne, Apple 1 hits the market!
  • 1977: Apple released the Apple II computer.
  • 1980: Apple was listed on stock exchange.
  • 1984: Apple released the Macintosh computer, the first small computer with a graphical UI.
  • 1985: Jobs resigned from Apple and started a new company called NeXT computer, which developed the first computer to become a world wide web server.
  • 1986: Bought a division of Lucasfilm that became Pixar Animated Studios.
  • 1988: NeXT Computer released its first computer.
  • 1995: Became CEO and president of Pixar, after very successful release of "Toy Story".
  • 1996: Apple bought NeXT, Jobs returned to Apple as an advisor.
  • 1997: Jobs became CEO of Apple, Apple Store was created.
  • 1998: Apple released the iMac.
  • 2001: iTunes was introduced by Apple, only available on Mac. Later in the year the revolutionary iPod was released.
  • 2003: iTunes became windows compatible.
  • 2004: Jobs went into surgery to have a cancerous tumor removed from his pancreas.
  • 2005: Apple released iPod Shuffle and Nano models.
  • 2006: Jobs joined Disney's board as top shareholder after Disney purchased Pixar.
  • 2007: Apple released iPod Touch and also the the first iPhone. Jobs was also inducted into the California Hall of Fame that year.
  • 2008: The App Store was introduced.
  • 2009: Jobs went on a leave of abscence for 6 months due to medical reasons.
  • 2010: Apple released the first iPad
  • 2011: Jobs unveiled the iPad 2 in San Francisco. He also made his final public speech at Apple's Worldwide Developers Conference in June. Apple became the most valuable company in the USA in August and shortly after that Jobs resigned his position as CEO, becoming chairman. On October 5th, 2011 Steve Jobs passed away at the age of 56 after a long battle with pancreatic cancer.
